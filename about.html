About Me

Hello! I am Marie Taoko. I am originally from France, and I moved to Montreal for my studies.
Now, I am doing a bachelor’s in design at Concordia University, and I work as a graphic designer in various projects.

My purpose as a graphic designer is to welcome and appreciate everyone in my work. I aim to work with honesty, sharing, diversity, inclusivity, and accessibility for all people to feel acknowledged, understood, considered, appreciated, and be able to identify to my designs.
My surroundings, passions, acquired knowledge, and lived experiences are at the base of my practice. They guided me towards the values that reflect who I am as a person and as a designer.
