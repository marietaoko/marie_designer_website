Body Parts Make Us Who We Are

I have always taken an interest in the human body, its functions, and its physilogy.

With this series, I wanted to demonstrate how this assembly of cells, organs, body vessels, body fluids, bones, nerves… create these systems that make us live, that make us who we are. For me, the human body is one of the most incredible designs on earth, and it is all created by nature. It gives us a simple idea of what nature is capable of.
