Art Volt Collection Catalogue

Collaborative project with Vanessa Senécal, Anastasia Dalalakis, Paloma Rojas-Linares, Karlo Roncal, and Ainaz Nejat.

This project is the first edition of the Art Volt Collection catalogue. The Art Volt Collection is a not-for-profit artwork rental and sales service that supports Concordia's Fine Arts alumni in gaining exposure and launching their careers. Our aim was to create a catalogue that would follow Art Volt's clean and minimalistic vision, but also be an engaging design piece that would celebrate the Collection's emerging artist and artworks.
